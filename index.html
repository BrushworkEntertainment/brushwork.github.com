HALF-LIFE: REFLECTION

Half-Life: Reflection is a modification for Half-Life 2.

You play as programmer in a nuclear test facility when all of a sudden one of the reactors faces a critical error.
Did you cause it? Was it a mistake in your code?
It doesn`t matter. You know you have to deactivate the reactor to save the facility and the residents of the surrounding area.
But what you`ll find will proove to be a much larger mystery.

ABOUT BRUSHWORK

Brushwork is an unofficial, independent game-development studio "founded" in 2022.

We are a team of enthusiastic developers that want to tell engaging, rich stories using original gameplay.
We are currently developing Half-Life: Reflection.
